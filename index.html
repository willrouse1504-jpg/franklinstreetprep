Frequently asked questions

Helpful answers before we begin.

Do you offer in-person and online tutoring?

Yes. Sessions are available in the Chapel Hill area and online. Availability and meeting location are confirmed during the consultation.

How is a tutoring plan created?

We begin with goals, timing, academic history, and available test results. From there, we prioritize the skills and strategies that will have the greatest impact.

How long are sessions?

Most sessions are 60 or 90 minutes. The recommended format depends on the student's age, goals, and preparation timeline.

When should test preparation begin?

Many students benefit from starting 8–16 weeks before a target test date. A longer runway may be appropriate for larger score goals or busy schedules.

Do families receive progress updates?

Yes. Families receive concise updates on completed work, progress, and recommended next steps.
